Junior Accounting Associate (Shaun Raymond)


Shaun Raymond U.S. Tax Services Inc., is a fast growing CPA firm who is looking for a junior accounting associate to join our highly qualified tax team. The eligible candidate should possess the following skills and attributes:

  • Ability to work in a dynamic environment, both independently and in a team setting
  • Excellent oral and written communication skills
  • Attention to detail and accuracy
  • Strong computer skills, including MS Office
  • Good time management and ability to meet deadlines
  • Exposure to U.S. and Canadian tax, personal and corporate is an asset
  • Exposure to U.S. and Canadian notice to reader financial statements is an asset
  • Working towards an accounting degree or equivalent (with the future goal of obtaining their CPA designation)
